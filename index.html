Ethical Hacking

IEEE MSB presents a workshop on Ethical Hacking in association with Azure Skynet on 22nd and 23rd of February. An Ethical Hacker exposes vulnerabilities in software to help business owners fix those security holes before a malicious hacker discovers them. In this workshop, you learn all about Ethical hacking with loads of live hacking examples to make the subject matter clear.
